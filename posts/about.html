About

Data Science @ UCSC was started in January 2019 with a mission to help UC Santa Cruz students learn practical data science, analytics and machine learning skills.

Meetings

Weekly meeting: Thursdays 5pm-6:30pm.

Additional meetings: Reading groups and specific projects hold extra meetings in the week.

Google Calendar: datascienceucsc@gmail.com. Click to subscribe and stay up-to-date on all of our events.

Joining

We communicate through Slack at datascienceucsc.slack.com. You can join by clicking here. Additionally, you can email us at ucscdatascience@gmail.com, or message us on Instagram @datascienceucsc.

We welcome students from all majors, though you’ll have an easier time if you already have some quantitative and/or programming background!
